The India vs Australia Super Eight clash in the ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2024 was a thrilling encounter that saw Australia emerge victorious. The match, played on June 24, 2024, at the Darren Sammy National Cricket Stadium in Gros Islet, St. Lucia, was a closely fought contest that went down to the wire.  India, batting first, posted a respectable total of 167/7, with Virat Kohli's century being a key highlight.  Australia's chase was interrupted by rain, but they eventually reached the target of 168 with two balls to spare, thanks to a crucial knock by Glenn Maxwell.  The match was a testament to the quality of both teams and the high stakes involved in the Super Eight stage of the tournament.
